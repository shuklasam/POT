Price Optimization Tool – Hiring Assessment

About Client
A leading global enterprise seeks to improve and optimize its pricing strategy through the development of a Price Optimization Tool. This will allow users to input product data, analyse pricing trends, and receive optimal pricing recommendations.

Problem Statement
In the digital era, efficient pricing is crucial for businesses to stay competitive. The client requires a web application that enables business users to determine optimal pricing strategies based on demand forecasts and market conditions.

The goal is to create a multi-functional interface that addresses these needs effectively, integrating several functionalities while maintaining ease of use and performance efficiency.

Specific Challenges
* Dynamic User Roles and Permissions
o Implement a flexible user authentication and authorization system with dynamic role assignment (admin, buyer, supplier, custom roles).

* Product Management
o Securely manage product data with robust search and filtering, while adhering to data privacy regulations.

* Demand Forecast Integration
o Enable visualization of product demand trajectory and forecasts.

* Pricing Optimization
o Recommend optimized product prices based on business inputs.

Functional Requirements
PART A: Product Management
* Create and Manage Products
o Create, view, update, and delete products.
o Product attributes: name, category, cost price, selling price, description, stock available, units sold.
* Search and Filter Products
o Advanced search by product name.
o Filters (e.g., category).

PART B: Demand Forecast & Pricing Optimization
* Demand Forecast
o Show demand forecasts for products.
o Visualize forecasted demand vs selling price on a linear plot.
* Pricing Optimization
o Display optimized prices in tabular format along with product details.

Technical Requirements
* User Authentication & Authorization
o User registration/login with email verification.
o Role-based access control for different user roles.

* Backend
o Python (Django/Flask/FastAPI) or Node.js.
o Focus on scalability and security.

* Frontend
o React.js or Angular with responsive UI.
o Data visualization using Chart.js / D3.js.

* Database
o Relational DB (PostgreSQL/MySQL).
o Apply normalization and indexing for performance.

* Code Quality
o Well-structured, modular, and documented.
o Include a README.md with setup and project overview.

* UI/UX
o Clean, intuitive, and responsive design.
o Clear visualization of demand forecasts and price recommendations.
